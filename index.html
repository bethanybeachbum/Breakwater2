Full Moon Over The Ocean

Rough waves and stong winds pummeled our beach for a couple of days in late September and early October, causing the beach sand to be pulled out to sea. The high tide has been at the dune fence most days but the dune fence and dune remain safely intact. This natural occurance happens every fall or winter and the beach typically builds back up within weeks.
