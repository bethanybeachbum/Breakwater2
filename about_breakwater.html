About the Breakwater Beach Subdivision

Breakwater Beach subdivision is a enclave of 41 homes along the most beautiful stretch of Delawares Atlantic coast beach. With the Indian River Inlet on one side and the Atlantic Ocean on the other, every day is a beautiful sunrise and sunset.
